As a dyslexic scientist I believe science should be "of, by and, for" the people, not about them.
I strive to contribute to making (dyslexia) science accessible to all, especially to the communities it aims to serve. Therefore I participate in science outreach and public facing talks.

---

[![Watch the video](images/TEDx_thumbnail.png)](https://youtu.be/yjRxxTPduZg?si=MYLnVUNwebpZPD0J)
